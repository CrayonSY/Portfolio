About

  • 178cm / 70–75kg
  • Born 1998, Fukuoka, Japan
  • Based in Tokyo, Japan

Shinnosuke Yasuda was born in 1998 in Fukuoka, Japan. He received his Bachelor Degree in Art Practice at the University of California, Berkeley. He sets his creative identity between being an artist and a programmer.

While working as a freelance programmer, he spent the first half of his college career majoring in Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering and finished all of the lower-division Math and Physics courses, then the second half majoring in Art Practice. He explores the coexistence and co-prosperity between the real and virtual world, and pursues unusual sensational user experiences.

Another point of his works is dimensionality. Many of them transform between 2D and 3D, and change with time and viewer interaction — interactive changes he has advocated as "the change along the 5th dimension."
